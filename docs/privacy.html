Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

Nothing. CardCue does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

What stays on your device

The card names and notes you enter are stored locally on your iPhone. If you have iCloud enabled, CardCue also keeps a private backup copy in your iCloud account (Apple's iCloud Key-Value storage) so your data can be restored if you delete and reinstall the app. This backup is accessible only to your iCloud account — CardCue has no server, and we never see, receive, or have access to this data.

What we never access

CardCue does not access your bank accounts, financial data, location, contacts, camera, or any other device data.

Analytics & tracking

CardCue contains no analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.

Third parties

CardCue has no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or data-sharing agreements.

Contact

Questions? Reach us via the support page.
